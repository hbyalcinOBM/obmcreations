Artic for Art

Project information

  • Name: Artic for Art
  • Category: Mobile Application
  • Industry: Art and Shop

Artic is a special mobile application that makes art lovers addicted to itself. In our app, you can see priceless paintings of other people and put your paintings up with deadline of the auction. In addition to these, you surely do all of these on user-friendly screen thanks to OBM for All :).

How you use Artic for Art:

-Put your paintings up for auction with deadline of the auction
-Check highest bid of any painting and time left.
-Bid for paintings
-Make process of keeping up the art trend easy.
