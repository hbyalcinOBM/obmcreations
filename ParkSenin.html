ParkSenin

Project information

  • Name: ParkSenin
  • Category: Mobile Application
  • Industry: Park Management

This map-integrated app is designed for people in Istanbul to find autoparks and pay their fees online without dealing with anybody.

How you use ParkSenin:

-Open the Map
-Find car park closest to you
-Use car park with QR Code without speaking to any body
-Pay for that online -You are done chasing for car park around
